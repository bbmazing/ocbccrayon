Error recovery — the make-or-break

Design for being misunderstood

1
Detect low confidence
notice the model isn't sure — don't plough ahead
2
Ask a clarifying question
“Did you mean transfer, or balance?”
3
Allow easy rephrase
let the user say it again, differently
4
Escalate to a human
after repeated failure — with context
Graceful fallback separates good assistants from frustrating ones.

Wrap & Q&A

Design the conversation first

A model isn't a product — a conversation is. And the only way to know it works is to test it.

What we covered

  • Natural interaction: model real conversation
  • Context: memory = perceived intelligence
  • Voice UX: confirm, recover, hand off, multimodal
  • Capabilities + navigational deeplinks
  • A great turn: voice, visible tools, quick replies, feedback

The overarching rule

  • Test with real users early
  • Wizard-of-Oz · conversation logs · fallout analysis
  • People always say what you didn't script
